# Personal-Expense-Tracker
## 1. Project Title / Headline

**Personal Expense Tracker Dashboard – Expense Analysis & Financial Spending Insights**

---

## 2. Short Description / Purpose

The Personal Expense Tracker Dashboard is designed to monitor, analyze, and visualize personal spending patterns across different categories, payment methods, and time periods. It helps users identify major expense areas, track spending behavior, and make informed financial decisions to improve budgeting and savings. Based on the transaction data from March–April 2025, the dashboard provides a complete overview of expenses totaling **₹433,055.28**. 

---

## 3. Tech Stack

* **Power BI** – Dashboard creation and data visualization
* **Microsoft Excel / PDF Data Source** – Expense records storage
* **Power Query** – Data cleaning and transformation
* **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – KPI calculations and measures
* **Data Modeling** – Relationships and aggregation for reporting

---

## 4. Data Source

The dashboard uses personal transaction records containing:

* Date & Time
* Expense Category
* Description
* Amount
* Payment Mode

The dataset includes spending across categories such as Shopping, Education, Loan, Bills, Grocery, Entertainment, Transport, Food, Health, and Subscription. 

---

## 5. Features / Highlights

### Business Problem

Managing personal finances becomes challenging when expenses are spread across multiple categories and payment methods. Without proper tracking, users may overspend, miss budgeting opportunities, and struggle to identify unnecessary expenses.

---

### Goal of the Dashboard

* Monitor total spending.
* Identify highest expense categories.
* Track spending trends over time.
* Analyze payment method usage.
* Understand spending behavior by day and category.
* Support better budgeting and financial planning.

### Business Impact & Insights

#### Key Findings

1. **Shopping is the highest spending category**, contributing ₹63,220.47.
2. **Education expenses are substantial**, indicating investment in learning and development.
3. **Loan repayments account for over ₹51,000**, impacting disposable income.
4. **Cash remains the dominant payment method**, though digital transactions are widely used.
5. **Thursday and Friday show the highest spending activity**, useful for budget monitoring.
6. **Clothing, Course Fees, Books, and EMI payments are the major expense drivers.**

#### Financial Recommendations

* Set spending limits for Shopping expenses.
* Monitor recurring Loan and Subscription payments.
* Increase usage of digital payments for better tracking.
* Create monthly category-wise budgets.
* Review high-spending weekdays to control discretionary expenses.
* Track Education investments separately as long-term value-generating expenses.

### Conclusion

The Personal Expense Tracker Dashboard provides a comprehensive view of financial behavior, helping users understand where money is being spent, identify cost-saving opportunities, and make data-driven budgeting decisions. With total expenses of **₹433,055.28**, the dashboard highlights key spending patterns across categories, payment methods, descriptions, and time periods, enabling smarter personal finance management.
